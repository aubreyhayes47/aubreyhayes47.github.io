A Simple, Auditable Plugin System

Each plugin is declared with a manifest and confined by permissions. You choose the tools, define how they're wired, and can inspect every use.

{
  "name": "PDFSummarizer",
  "description": "Extracts and summarizes PDF content",
  "permissions": ["read:file", "use:llm"],
  "triggers": ["*.pdf"],
  "entry": "./run.py"
}
